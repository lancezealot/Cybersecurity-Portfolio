## About Me

### Who Am I?

Hello, I'm **John Reyl**, a Computer Science graduate with a strong passion for technology, problem-solving, and information security. While my current professional role is as a Medical Insurance Approvals Coordinator, where I manage insurance authorization processes, documentation review, and coordination between healthcare providers and insurance companies, my long-term career goal is to transition into the cybersecurity field.

My background in Computer Science has provided me with a solid foundation in networking, operating systems, programming, and information technology principles. Over time, I developed a strong interest in cybersecurity and have dedicated myself to continuous learning through self-study, hands-on labs, security research, and industry training.

I am currently expanding my cybersecurity expertise by studying topics such as network security, vulnerability assessment, threat detection, security operations, ethical hacking, and risk management. Through practical exercises and security-focused learning platforms, I have built a strong understanding of cybersecurity fundamentals and continue to develop the technical skills required to succeed in the industry.

My unique combination of analytical thinking, attention to detail, and professional experience in a highly regulated healthcare environment has strengthened my ability to assess risks, follow security-conscious processes, and work effectively under pressure. I am actively working toward a career in cybersecurity and am eager to apply my growing knowledge to help organizations protect their systems, data, and digital assets.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new security technologies, participating in cybersecurity labs and challenges, and staying updated on the latest trends and threats within the cybersecurity landscape.
